Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our Credit Repair FAQ section! Whether you're just starting your journey to a better credit score or looking to fine-tune your financial health, we're here to help. Dive into our top questions and answers to get the expert insights you need to take control of your credit today.

1. What is credit repair?

Credit repair involves disputing inaccuracies on your credit report, working to remove negative items, and improving your credit score through various strategies.

2. Can I repair my credit myself?

Yes, you can repair your credit yourself by obtaining your credit reports, identifying errors, and disputing them with the credit bureaus.

3. How long does credit repair take?

The duration varies, but it generally takes three to six months to see significant improvements, depending on the severity of the issues being addressed. Our averages are between fifteen to fourty-five days.

4. Is credit repair legal?

Yes, credit repair is legal, but be wary of companies that make unrealistic promises or charge upfront fees, as these can be signs of a scam.

5. How can I improve my credit score quickly?

To improve your credit score quickly, pay down high credit card balances, ensure timely payments, and avoid opening new accounts unnecessarily.
